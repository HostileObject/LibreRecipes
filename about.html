The Project

This project tries to showcase how modern web loads a bunch of useless scripts, putting a heavy, unwanted, easy-avoidable charge on the bandwith of the person loading the page. This website tries to demonstrate what you can do with only CSS and HTML: a totally functional website, that looks good, functions as espected and doesn't slow down nor your computer nor your network.

"Bloat"

Bloat is the part of a program/script/website/etc... that isn't required by the program to run and/or uses more memory, processing power, disk space. The term "Bloat" is quite subjective but we can agree that things like telemetry, functions that aren't related to the program's original service, and things like cookies can be considered bloat.

How to Contribute

Our GitHub has all the instructions and templates needed to add a recipe to the website. If you want to contribute but don't really know how even after reading the instructions email me at samueletonarini@gmail.com and I will happily answer. In our recipes pages we only accept the ingredient list, the explanation and a maximum of 5 photos. At the bottom of the page there's a section where the author can link to his website or other recipes. For more information visit our GitHub page.
